# AffectAtlas — Product context

**Register: brand.** This repo is the marketing/landing site for the AffectAtlas app. The
deliverable is the impression. The site must clear the bar of its audience: working designers.

## What the product is
AffectAtlas is an Android app (coming to Google Play) that turns an emotion into a complete,
research-backed design system. Pick a feeling, get colour tokens, type specimens, spacing, motion,
depth, shape, layout, iconography, do's/don'ts, word clouds, and emotional context. Built by
**Three Eyed Emu**.

- **29 emotions**: 8 Plutchik primaries, 8 blended dyads, 13 deepened nuances. (Love has 3
  variants: Love, Passion, Lust.) Each runs at 3 intensities → 87 advisory entries.
- **11 design dimensions** per advisory.
- **9 export formats**: CSS, Tailwind, Figma Tokens, Swift, Kotlin, SCSS, JSON, W3C Design Tokens,
  AI Prompt.
- **29 typographic identities** drawn from 58 Google Fonts; every emotion reads differently.
- Free to explore. Exports unlock with a one-time purchase.
- Grounded in Plutchik's psychoevolutionary theory, 40+ years of emotion research.

## Audience
Designers and design-minded builders who already feel the gap between "technically correct" and
"emotionally resonant." They judge craft instantly. The site's job is to *be the proof* that this
tool understands feeling, colour, and type.

## Voice
Scientific but alive. Confident, plain, a little poetic. Never hypey. The atlas metaphor (a printed
reference of plates) is the organising idea.

## Anti-references (what this site must never read as)
- Generic dark SaaS landing page with one accent colour and glass cards.
- AI-default typography (Inter / Instrument Sans / DM / Fraunces families).
- Identical icon-card grids, pill badges above every heading, gradient text.
